It appeared , that about 17 years since , the Prisoner , the deceased , and two more , were drinking at the Three Tuns in Shandois-street , a discourse arose amongst them about chusing Parliament Men , which was debated so hot between them , that the Lye was given , and Swords were drawn , and in the fray the deceased received the aforesaid Wound . The Constable declared that he did ask the Prisoner , if he could tell how long it was since he killed the deceased , and he replied , That it was about 17 years since . The Prisoner alleged . That the deceased threw a Glass of Wine in his Face , catcht him by the Perriwig and thrust his Fingers in his Eyes . It appeared that there was no premeditated malice between them .
