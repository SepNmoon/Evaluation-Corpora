Bullstrode Nover . 6 th 1781 . My Dear Duchess of Portland Having heard that the Stag was taken at Gerrards croſs & that the Chace ended there & the weather being so bad , I was very sorry I did not know it time enough to offer Miſs Hamilton my Carriages but I hope His Majesty will do me the Honour to Command them whenever they can be of use . I am very Happy to hear His Majesty is well . I hope you are well I cant give up the hopes of seeing you here . Her Majesty has got a little cold but much yours I am My Dear Mrs Delany Your affectionate Humble Servant Miſs Hamilton To MC Portland The Queens Lodge
