My Dear Miſs Hamilton I feel I must go to Glanville tomorrow , my Spirits wont bear another Desolated Day in this House but I wish you coud bestow an Hour upon Me tomorrow Morning shoud the Day Prove Fair -- as I do n't Propose going before 5 o Clock -- our Dear Friend is Just gone believe me most affectionately yours -- -- MD I shared this afternoon with Mrs Sandford -- Mrs Delany July 25 th . -- 84 To Miſs Hamilton Clarges Street
