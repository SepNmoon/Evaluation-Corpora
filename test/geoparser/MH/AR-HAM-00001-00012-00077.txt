My dr Miſs Hamilton with many thanks for ye Long loan of ye Picture that you will receive with this permit me to repeat my Apologies for having kept it from you for many Months -- It is to my Misfortune that I have Reap 'd so little Advantage from it -- & I shall always consider ye having had so pretty a Production before me without having made any Use of it as an Additional melancholly Proof of my Capacity of throwing away many Aſsistances offer 'd me for a better Purpose -- This is Greek Hebrew to You who never were guilty of any Such thing -- So I will put an End to such an unintelligible & scrawl 'd Epistle -- only flattering myself that you will make out that I am always yr oblig 'd & sincere humble Sert H Finch Kew Sunday Night
