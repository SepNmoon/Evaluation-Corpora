My Dear Miſs Hamilton , I beg you to be so good to deliver the Enclosed to P Ernest & Pſs Elizabeth & hope you will indulge me with a Line to know how they all do , as well as yourself . Mr Feilding I doubt not has deliver 'd to you the Manuscript I charged him with Yesterday . with a Note from me . I have nothing material to add so will only say I am ever & most Affly . Yrs . C.Finch Ly Louisa begs to be kindly remember 'd to you I forgot to say to P. Ernest I will do what he desires as soon as I come to Town but can not from hence . Harleyford . March 26 th . 1780 To Miſs Hamilton Queens House London Hon Wm : Clayton
