Wednesday 8 th Sepr 1784 My Dear Miſs Hamilton I am so taken up with busineſs that I have not a moment to my self but you may depend upon my seeing you before I go I send you the pedestal for the Vase for the D of P—— which I am sure she will think well contrived it was Crighton my cabinet maker maker in in Kings Street Soho that made it & he can repair it & fit the glaſses as I intended if the Ducheſs chooses to employ him -- he is remarkable for obbs -- To day I am going will c sometime on Thursday . Yrs . ever & most affly . W : H . Uncle William Sepr . 8 th - . 84 Uncle Wm : Clarges Street
