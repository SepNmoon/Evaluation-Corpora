12 th . Novbr . 1782 My Dear friend As I Have not time to write more than a line I send ye . enclosed unseal 'd wch . You will peruse & then seal and send Your Servt . with it to Lady King s in Dover Street -- let me have Your wishes for my happineſs -- let me have Your friendship for to support my spirits on this important occasion -- Adieu I am agitated & not able to add more to You You may mention at Your table , some days hence that You hear I mean to quit ye . family as my health is not equal to ye . fatigue & that my friends have advised me to it & c & c -- Honle : Miſs Gunning S t : James 's Courtown
