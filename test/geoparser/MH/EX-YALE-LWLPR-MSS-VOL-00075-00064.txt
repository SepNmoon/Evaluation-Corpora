My Dear Friend I shall be glad to see you for an Hour on Monday -- and for the whole afternoon on Wednesday -- and hope to reward you for your goodneſs to me in treating you with our Dear Dutcheſs who I thank god is very well -- Nephew & Niece do n't come till Tuesday -- send me a dozen Proposals of the Milk Woman , or I shall Lose some favourable opportunity I have seen a great deal of the world since we met & I have been indeed but very indifferent am better and if I see you well and in Spirits -- I shall be st better -- going to Bed so good Night affectionately yours MD To Miss Hamelton Mrs Delany 22 Janry 85
