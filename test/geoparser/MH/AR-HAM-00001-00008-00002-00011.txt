March 29 th -- My dear Mrs. Dickenson I Hail yr arrival in Town th ' o I am sorry for the Cause which brings you amongst us -- I am so perpetually Engag 'd every Evening , that I can not go out on a Morning . not having Strength of Constitution Sufficient to Encounter such fatigue or I wd have made this Enquiry in person -- we all Unite in best Compliments & wishes for a Speedy recovery -- & I hope you have brought Mr Dickenson & yr sweet Girl in perffect Health -- yrs most truly DBlosset . Mrs Dickenson No 21 South Audly Street
