How miserable Madam , must I feel to give up Miſs Hamilton & her amiable Friends to night . Nothing but Illneſs & a Headach which ought not to remain on these shoulders for even a few hours , shod have prevent 'd the honor I promised myself in waiting upon you & marking with how much regard I am Mss Hamilton most Obedt . Servant Ag Vesey April the 6 th 1780
