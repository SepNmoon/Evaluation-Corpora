Janry 1784 Queens House Indeed the account you heard was very true , only with this difference that it is since Tuesday that I have been confined , I may say to my Bed , as I only get up for a little time , I am however thank God better , it is a Fever , & our good Friend Turton thinks of the intermitting kind , & aſsures me I shall soon be well -- God Bleſs you my dear & believe me most truly sensible of yr kindneſs & that I am & well Affly yr MCG --
