Dear Sir Mrs. Garrick is come to Town for a few hours only , she is very unwilling to depart without seeing her Blackamoor , and if you will be at home this morning about Eleven she will call on you in her way out of Town , just to ask how you do , she is obliged to return directly to Hampton Yours half asleep H More 7 o clock Monday Morn Mr. Dickenson at Mrs. Glover s Albemarle Street
