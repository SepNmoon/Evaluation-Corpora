My Dear Miſs Hamilton I shall only attempt adding a few Lines to thank you for yours , & for the enclosed Acct . of Mr & Mrs Graham 's safe arrival , which I sincerely rejoice in . I beg you will deliver the enclosed for her Royl . Hſs . from whom you will hear a very good Account of Prince Alfred . Yrs . ever most Sincerely CFinch Deal Castle June 17 th . 1782 Miſs Hamilton Queens Lodge Windsor
