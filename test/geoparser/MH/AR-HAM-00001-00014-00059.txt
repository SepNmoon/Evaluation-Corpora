Queens House Tues day Morg . I beg my Dear , you will present my Humble Duty to Her Majesty & acquaint her , that Dear Prince Alfred has had a tolerable Night , & is in good Spirits to Day . The Princeſs 's & Prince Octavius are very well & desire their Duty to their Majesties , & Love to the Princeſs 's Adieu my Dear Hammy Affly Yr- MCGoldsworthy -- Sent to Windsor Tuesday 7 th . May 1782
