My dear Friend Mrs. G 's Box will hold five in the front Row if you leave your Court hoop at home . A Man or so who can stand may do very well behind . You have only to deliver the Card at the Door and desire to be conducted to Mrs. Garrick 's Box -- no bribery or corruption be attempted . I hope to see you very soon .
