Green Street Leicester fields My Dear Sister , Maria & I are much flattered with your Congratulations . We are just returned to Town & dine at Lady Clavering 's . My Chariot & Servant shall wait your Commands about ½ past Seven & Shall carry You home again at Night . As to Ly Stormont s Note , I can only say we regret she can not attend the Presentation & shall not trouble her for her Chair . I ever am Your Affect . friend & Brother N—— this is wrote in great haste
