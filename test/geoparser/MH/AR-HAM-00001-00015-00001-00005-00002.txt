My dearest Friend , How are you -- in tolerable spirits I sincerely hope -- I send you a handcherchief for which you owe me 2/9 d -- Do not go to Hero 's on Sunday , because I can not my Father who has a particular reason for wishing me to dine at home , is gone to beg me off if you send an excuse now it will not seem concerted -- adieu I am very well --
