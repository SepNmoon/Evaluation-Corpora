Madam my Aunt desires me to say she is happy to find that you are willing to make one of her select cotterie , ( next Wednesday to dinner at 4 ' o'Clock ) who ' will all be happy with such an adition , as it is a party in Coy my Aunt desires you not to mention it . she begs her affectionate Compts . -- I am Madam with the truest Respect & esteem Your Obliged & Obedient Humb Sert . Georgina Mary Anne Port -- S t . James 's Place Febry . 27 th . Thursday 5 ' o'Clock --
