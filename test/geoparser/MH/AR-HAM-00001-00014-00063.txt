Novbr . 19 th - 1782 windsor The Queen has been so gracious to send me a Meſsage to advise me not to go out this Raw Cold day , will it be very inconvenient to you my dr to take my waiting this Evg . if it is not I really shall be obliged to you , I go on very well & do not doubt but that in a couple of Days I shall be quite bonny , sick or well Affly Yr MCG -- pt one -- do n't imagine I am worse by this Note , for I am not & fully intended coming & only am prevented by the Queen s gracious Meſsage
