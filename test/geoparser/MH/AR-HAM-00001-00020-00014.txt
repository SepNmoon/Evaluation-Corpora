Miſs tells me she has been impertinent & desires you to keep me in order , tell her for answer that it is impoſsible -- Your Picture is finished & is now upon the Road to you . I have desired the bearer of it Mr- Leslie ( Son to Lord Leven ) to deliver it to you in person , if he does , be civil to him . You will not find the likeneſs a good one , but it is the best I have & so you must e'en content yourself and do as the Galloway Wife did Adieu -- Alexander March 3 d 1779
