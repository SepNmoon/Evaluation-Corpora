Sir I have receiv 'd the favor of yours of the 11 th . of this Inst . from Northampton ; and this morning according to your desire I have paid one Thousand Pounds to your Bankers Meſsrs . Joseph Jones & Co . I likewise called upon Miſs Ann Clarke and paid her Twenty Five Pounds for both which Sums I have receiv 'd your Receipts . I beg you will present my best Compts . to Mrs. Dickenson & be aſsured that I am with great regard Dear Sir , Your faithful Humble Servt . Frederick Hamilton Bedford Square May 13 th . 1786
