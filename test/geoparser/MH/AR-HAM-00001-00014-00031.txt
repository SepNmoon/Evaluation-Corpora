Hopital des Invalides Monday Morg 15th Novbr . 1779 -- 9 'oClock My Dear Coughing & Sneezing I just write two lines to say that I am realy better I was sent to Bed at night last Night & am but just up now sitting by my own Fire s ide , Mou Mou , is sitting at the other side half dead with this same Cold , Mrs Hagerdon so hoarse she can not speak so that the Queen & Princeſ 's are perfectly well attended , God Bleſs you my Drt & believe me Affly Yr MCG Mou Mou sends her best love to Chi Chi .
