Copy of my letter to Ly Charlotte Finch My Dearest Madam I have in my poſseſsion a Pavilion made by the Nuns at Lisbon which Miſs Cathcart brought over to present to Her Royal Highneſs Princeſs Elizabeth . If you think it proper I will have it conveyed to the Queens House & placed in Mlle . Moula 's room , as it is too large to trouble you with ; I did mention it to her Majesty in the Summer & the Queen did not object to Her Royal Highneſs receiving it as a present from Miſs I have the honor of being My Dearest Lady Charlotte Your sincere & affte Friend Mary Hamilton Clarges Street March 1783
