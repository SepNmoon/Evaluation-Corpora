Mr : Hope preſents his respectful Compts : to Mrs : & Miſs Hamilton . He is happy to hear they are ſafe return 'd to Northton . He intends paying his respects to them in perſon , in the afternoon ; if they do not prevent him . -- In the mean while , he begs Miſs Hamilton 's acceptance of the Companion he promised to the laſt Drawing he gave her -- It will ſhew he has amused himself , in her Abſence , with other Things than Concerts , Scandal , Excurſions & Verſes . -- It has little merit than being the only Drawing he has done for eleven years paſt , which , & the Goodwill with which it is given , muſt plead with her for all it 's Imperfections . --
