Wensday I am oblig 'd to go to Lonn this Morning my Dear Madam , so that I can not wait on You in the Evening , but ſhou 'd be very glad of the favor of seeing You Here , which I must not hope for ( I doubt ) but to Morrow I depend on seeing You & Mr. Dickenson at 4 o clock , & I have taken the liberty to tell yr Friend Mr W—— who He wou 'd find if He were graciously dispos 'd towards our petit Couvert Bon Jour To Mrs. Dickenson Honble . Mrs. Boscawen 1 st . Octbr . 1788
