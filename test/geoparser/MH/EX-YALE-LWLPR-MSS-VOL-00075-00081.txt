Dear Madam Miſs Port is gone to Church -- I shall therefore take upon me to answer yr . kind Note which I have the Happyneſs of being able to do in as satisfactory a way as the time will permit -- Mrs. Delany certainly continue gradually mending -- Dr. Turton s expreſsion this Morning was “ I have the greatest reason to believe now that we shall have our Old Friend restored to us -- but for that purpose it is absolutely neceſsary to keep her as quiet as poſsible ” -- which advice you may be sure we most strictly observe -- & hope & trust her very valuable Life will yet be spared to her Family & Friends -- kind Compts to Mr. Dickenson & am Yrs . very sincerely BDewes
