My dear Miſs Hamilton , I hope you are disengag 'd next Sunday Evening , & that you will give us the pleasure of yr company to meet Mrs Carter , & if you will give her leave she will call on you in the Coach & carry you here at Night I was in hopes some of these fine Mornings wd have induc 'd you to take a Walk towards Dover Street : Adieu & believe me My dear Madam -- yr ever faithful & c DBloſset Dover Street Thursday Evening -- 20 th March 1783 Miſs Hamilton Clarges Street --
