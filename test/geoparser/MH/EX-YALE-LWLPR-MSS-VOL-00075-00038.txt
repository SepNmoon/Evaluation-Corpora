Tuesday Morning St James Place Mrs Delany 's kindest Compts to Miſs Hamelton and is much oblig 'd to Her for the Pleasing Entertainment the Poem gave Her last night -- and will tell Miſs H—— more of Her mind when she has the Pleasure of seing Her -- which she hopes will be -- soon -- why not this afternoon -- when she will make another Friend Happy as well as Her self -- to morrow Mrs D Expects Mrs & Miſs Port -- which will be a busy day with Her -- 25 Jan 83 Delany Portland
