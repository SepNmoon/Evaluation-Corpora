My Dear Madam Am not I to have the Pleasure to carry You to Morrow ye 9 th . to drink early Tea with Lady Cremorne ? I think to go about 5 . & am so particularly engag 'd at Dinner that I can not ask You to be of my Party , but will call You where ever You order me , & am always yr Obedient F.Boscawen Audley Street 8 May To Mrs. Dickenson North Audley Street
