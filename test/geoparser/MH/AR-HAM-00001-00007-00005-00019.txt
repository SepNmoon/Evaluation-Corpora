My dear Miſs Hamilton I quite long to see you & wish you wd . spend Tuesday Evening with me either here or at yr . own rooms or if you wd . like it at Ly D 's who says you said something about Tuesday to her & so did I but I 'm afraid of all yr . days going without my seeing you shall we conclude you have been too busy to call on us . we shall take the first opportunity we can to wait on Mrs. Hamilton Yrs . sincerely S. Feilding Ly . D . desired me to send these Franks . Sunday E Miſs Hamilton S t . James 's
