My Dear Miſs Hamilton , I was just writing a Note to Miſs G . to excuse my Attendance at the Queen 's House , & have given a Description of myself that I will not repeat to you , as you have ſeen Devaynes , who says 't is absolutely neceſsary for me to take some care of myself & keep house a little . I am sorry to be obliged to submit to it , but I really feel it neceſsary myself . Yrs . ever Sincerely CFinch Miſs Hamilton
