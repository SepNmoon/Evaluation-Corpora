Ma chere Amie Mrs. G . hopes that You and the Blackamoor will come here on friday morning to breakfast at ten o'clock , and devote the whole day to us , which is to be a blackguard Holiday . Let us have your answer to Night as we go to Hampton to morrow for a day or two . I had this book in my pocket last Night but cou 'd not give it You . Yrs . ever H More Mrs. Dickenson 20 th . April 1788
