My dear Miſs Hamilton I send you a Letter from our dear friend Mrs. Carter . I shall write about friday or Saturday if you chuse to send me a Letter for her I will carry it to her thr ' o the Medium of my Frank -- I think you are a very Naughty Miſs . never to come near me -- I wish I was Wiser & better for yr sake that you might like me more -- but such as I am I will be yrs . Affecly DBloſset Tuesday ye . 8 th - Miſs Hamilton
