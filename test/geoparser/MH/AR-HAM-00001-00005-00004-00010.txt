Dr Sr . If you should have any wish to see a Representation of Lord Neilson 's Victory I shall be very glad to have the pleasure of your Company today . I understand it is much admired & that the Great Officers & c attend . this under the direction of a Gentleman of my Acquaintance -- it begins at -- 1 . oClock in Fleet Street -- If you chuse it I will call on you a little after 12 oClock . -- Yours sincerely & c Warwick S t . James 's Place Wednesday May 15 th 1799
