Thursday Evening 14 th August 1783 A Thousand thanks to you My Dear niece for your kind eagerneſs to see me I am just come from St . James 's tired to death & very hungry -- so shall only say that I will meet you any day you please at my Brother Frederick s as I live at the Hotel King Street if I do go to Scotland it will not be thes ' e ten days & I am not determined to go -- your tired hungry but affectionate Uncle Wm : Hamilton let my Br . know the day you intend to come to Town if not inconvenient but beg you will not put yourself to inconveni ence Thursday past 5 & no dinner yet To The Honble . Miſs Hamilton
