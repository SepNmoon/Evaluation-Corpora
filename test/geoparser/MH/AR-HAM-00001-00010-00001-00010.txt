My dearest Miranda I have only five minutes to write on the subject of y our dear Mother 's Health -- she is much the same as when you left her -- the day before yesterday very chearful -- she had not a good night on monday -- she sends her Love to you -- Mrs. C & Kitty & I sa were with her last night . My Sisters desire their Love . I am My dear Miranda Yours with the sincerest Affecti on Anna Maria -- Wednesday morng . /
