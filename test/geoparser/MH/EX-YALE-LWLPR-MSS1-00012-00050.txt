Berkeley Square June 11 th . 1788 . Dear Madam if you & Mr Dickenson like to see the Play at Richmond house on Saturday next , I have asked Gen. Conway for tickets for you both , & he has promised me them . It will be the last performance this year . Be so good as to let me know before dinner , if you shoud not be at home when I send this . yr most obed . Sert HorWalpole Honble Horace Walpole June 1788
