My Dear Miſs Hamilton , They have prevail 'd on me at last to be of the Water Party to Hammersmith to convey Lady Louisa Clayton there , & as the Evening is so fine that the Princeſses will hardly come in long before their bed time , I hope they will excuse me I may be back before their Majestys return , if not , & I shd . be ask 'd for , you will be so good to say where I am gone . Ever Affly . Yrs . C.Finch 7 o'Clock Wednesday Evening To Miſs Hamilton
