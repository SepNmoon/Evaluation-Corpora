Dear Miſs Hamilton I am very sorry that It will not be in My Power to Attend you to Wandsworth Hill , till Thursday ; If that Day will be agreable to you , will call on you before 12 o'clock ; -- I shall be glad to Hear that you have fixed on a House , as you was to see three , this Morning ; & to Hear the dreaded Interview is over , & I am sure to the satisfaction of Both Parties , I Hope Robt . Behaved well ; I am dear Miſs Hamilton , Yrs . Most Affecly -- Frances Harpur Monday
