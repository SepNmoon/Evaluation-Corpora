Accept my Dearest Mrs Dickenson the sincerest wishes an affectionate heart can form for your mutual Happineſs & I beg you will make my Compts & Congratulations acceptable to Mr Dickenson I am impatient to see you & hear of your future plans & flatter my self I may be a partaker of them , tho ' at present the amendment is so slow I can hardly perceive I gain strength my Dear Friend I can no longer hold my pen but most affectly & Faithfully Yrs MC Portland Recd . at Fulham from ye . Ducheſs Dowr . of Portland June 25 th . 1785
