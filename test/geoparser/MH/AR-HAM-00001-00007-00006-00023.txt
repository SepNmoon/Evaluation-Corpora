Windsor Castle Feb : 2 . Accept my dear Madam the sincere Congratulations of an old Friend upon an Event which from what I have heard on the subject must afford you the highest degree of satisfaction . Mrs. Fisher unites with me in these Congratulations which we beg to offer to Mr. Dickenson also , & to the Bride . Our settling in London is a matter of great uncertainty not depending upon ourselves . I fear it will not be before the middle or the end of the present month .
