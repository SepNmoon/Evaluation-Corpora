Dear Miſs Hamilton , The Dſs of Ancaster has brought the enclosed Card for you , which you are to send to the Pantheon where they will give your Servant the Ticket in exchange for it . I repeat a thousand Wishes of amusement to you from Yrs . Sincerely CFinch Kew . June 13 th . Sunday Night .
