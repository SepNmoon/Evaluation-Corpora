My Dear Sister , Read the inclosed if You can . It was hastily copied from a Paper of the Chancellor 's which he exhibited , as the Intelligence given in the French Papers arrived to day . As I did not reach home till past Six , I have this moment dined , & am now set in for the Evening , to read the Papers in a Scotch Cause , therefore , You must console Yourself , the best way You can , as I shall not see Mr. D . & You this Evening , having habited myself in my Night Gown . Truly Your Affect . Brother N—— Return the Paper by the Bearer Mrs- Dickenson 7 Dover Street
