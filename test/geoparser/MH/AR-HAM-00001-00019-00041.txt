London May 5 th - 1773 I write two lines to My dear Ward just to ſay I got hers from Canterbury & I am quite well thank God again . I propose sending you a Dog by the Coach to morrow which I hope you 'll like her Name is Cloe & I am told she dances & plays many tricks but I 'll leave that for you to find out , I had a Puppy of my own Dog for you but a horse broke its Leg so I was oblig 'd to drown it , and a Lady gave me this one for you , if you write soon derect under Cover to the Earl of Panmure Chesterfield Street May fair London as His Lordship will always know where to ſend it to me . Ld Cathcart & family leave this to morrow for Scotland Adieu My dearest Mary believe me yours most Affctly- N——r remember me to Mrs Hamilton
