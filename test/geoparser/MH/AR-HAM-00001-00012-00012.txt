My Dear Miſs Hamilton All here rejoiced in your good Account of yr Royl Charges , & I hope you will be able to continue it . I am writing past 11 o Clock & consequently half asleep so you must not expect any particulars from me only the general kind remembrances of all here . I beg my Affate . Duty to & Princeſses & remain Ever your most Affcte . & Faithful CFinch Windsor Saturday Night 1779
