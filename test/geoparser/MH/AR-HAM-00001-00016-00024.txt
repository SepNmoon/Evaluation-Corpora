Dear Miſs Hamilton I Had Company when your Note arrived , am sorry you thought It neceſsary to Make any Apology for not coming to Me on Thursday -- the Weather was so Bad I was very glad you staid w . Lady Stormont ; I shall w . pleasure go on w . the Knotting for Mrs. Delany -- pray tell Her so ; -- I will come to You next Friday -- If agreable to You ; and If I dont Hear any thing to the contrary -- I am quite Well ; excepting a Cold in my Eyes , they are rather Better ; I am dear Miſs Hamilton , ever Yrs . Most Affecly- Frances Harpur Saturday 14 th -
