Dear Madam I am happy to announce to you the glad tidings of Lady Mansfield having been this morning safely brought to bed of a fine & stout Boy , who with its dear mother , I am happy to add , are at present as well as We could wish . -- I beg my best respects to Mr. Dickenson & am Dear Madam Your very faithful & Obedient Humb le Servant Robt . F : Greville Monday Decr . 1 st : London December One 1800 Mrs : Dickenson Leighton Buzzard Bedfordshire free RFGreville
