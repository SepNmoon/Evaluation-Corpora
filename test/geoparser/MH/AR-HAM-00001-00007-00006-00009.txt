Mr. Fisher presents his best Compliments to Miſs Hamilton & begs the favour of her to inform the Queen , that the two Drawings which her Majesty was graciously pleased to command some time since are finished . Mr. Fisher wishes to know in what manner he should convey them to her Majesty . Shall he send them to Miſs Hamilton , & beg the favour of her to present them ? Mr Roberts is so obliging as to take this , & will wait for Miſs Hamilton s answer . Friday Morn : 6 th . Septr . 1782
