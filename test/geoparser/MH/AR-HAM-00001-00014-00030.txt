Windsor 14 th Novbr . 1779 My Frd I am sure as I know you , you will be glad to know that I have really had a very good Night , & am as much better as I could reasonably expect , I do not pretend to say I am quite well , for my Cough is at times very troublesome , & nothing but Patience that Sovereign remedy to all Human ills can get the better of it . I write to you from my Bed where I have been ordered to stay this Morg am just going to Breakfast with Mou Mou -- God Bleſs you my dearest & keep you form these Colds & every other complaint of Mind & Body my Love to Chi Chi Affly Yours MCGoldsworthy ¼ before 9 Sunday Morg kiſs the dear Children --
