Shown Mrs. Cooper My Dear Madam I send You the incomparable little Book for Your pricious Louisa . I send You also the Letter which You say Lord Stormont wishes to see , but having no Copy of it I will beg You to return it when I have the Pleasure to wait on You on Tuesday after Breakfast . Mean time & always my Dear Madam very much Yours FBoscawen Saturday To Mrs. Dickenson Honble- Mrs. Boscawen
