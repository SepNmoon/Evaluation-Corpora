My dear Friend -- I am under the disagreeable neceſsity of diſsappointing you & myself this Evening but I have just received an unsollicited Ticket for the Ladies annual dinner at the Catch Club , which has been procured for me with great difficulty so that I can not refuse it , & fear I shall not be back in the Evening time enough to come to you therefore do not stay at home , tho ' If they break -- up tolerably early I shall take my chance of find ing you -- adieu my dear Friend affecly : yours C.M.G . Friday To Mrs Dickenson
