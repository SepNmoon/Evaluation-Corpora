Mrs. Boscawen presents her Compliments to Miſs Hamilton & informs Her that Mr Sweatman has appointed Friday next at One o ' clock , for the private View of the Ants & c which Miſs Hamilton wish 'd to partake of . If therefore it is convenient to Her to do so , Mrs. Boscawen will have the Honour to call Her abt a quarter after 12 next Friday the 18 th . . As it is a good Way to the Scene of Action ( viz Clements Inn ) & they are to go first to Lady de Clifford 's in order to accompany her Ladyp . & her Party thither . Audley Street Monday 14 th May 1781 PS If any thing shd prevent Miſs Hamilton 's going She will be so good to acquaint Mrs B .
