1803 Announcement of death of Sir Wm . Hamilton Dear Madam Lady Hamilton received your Letter & is sensible of your kindneſs . I need not say she is unable at present to acknowlege it , when I tell you , that I have the painful task of communicating our Severe loſs this Morning at 10 oClock -- He died as he lived . Sensible & composed to the last .. we unite in Compts . to Mr Dickenson & I am Dear Madam Your obedt & Faithful Sert C F. Greville
