Sunday 22 d : Feby . Dſs of P . s birthday Dear Madam my Aunt desires I will tell you , that she hopes you will come this Evening to Tea or any time after 7 ' o'Clock for she thinks it an age since she had that happiness , & you will meet company that she thinks you will have no objection too . & AAunt D . wishes for no other addition . & I am Dear Madam Your much Obliged Humb . Servt . G.M.A.Port To Miſs Hamilton
