S t . James 's . March 4 th . Tuesday My Dear Miſs Hamilton On mentioning to her Majesty the Pavillion Miſs Cathcart wishes to present to Pſs Elizabeth , she said She recollected the Circumstance very well , & that Miſs Cathcart was to have brought it herself , but as She is not in Town the Queen desires you wd . send it to me at S t James 's . I am sorry I have not yet had it in my power to call on you in a Morning , but will take the very first Opportunity of doing so , & you will allow when I tell you my various & new Occupations , that I have been fully employ 'd . I am Ever Yrs . most Sincerely C.Finch
