Mrs. Delany s Answer -- Windsor Novbr . 30 th 1786 Mrs. D : presents her Compts . to Mr. W . & Her acknowledgments for ye . very kind & acceptable present He has made her ( wch . she recd . last night ) of his new Edition of His anecdotes on Painting -- nor is she insensible of ye . Honor done her in mentioning her name in so ingenious & valuable a work wth . so much delicacy as to reconcile her to a Publication that wd . have been rather Painful from any other hand .
