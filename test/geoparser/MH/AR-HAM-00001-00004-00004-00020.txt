Sat Night 4 th August Septr . 1784 My D . Miſs H Busineſs has so increased upon me I have not had a moment to call upon you it is impoſsible to do more than one thing well at a time I shall therefore content myself tomorrow with calling upon you about One o'clock to go to Mrs. Garrick s if Mr. Walpole s was in the way I shoud not dislike calling upon him but I do not think of Wandsworth Hill yrs ever . W.H . -- Uncle Wm- Septr . 84 Miſs Hamilton Clarges Street
