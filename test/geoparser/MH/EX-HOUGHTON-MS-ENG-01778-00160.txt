Mrs. Ord who is much engaged on friday wishes to have the breakfasting Party at your house on thursday -- if you settle it so Mrs. G . and will be agreeable ; but this misfortune will attend it that Thursday can produce no Hampton cream . If we hear nothing to the contrary we shall come on thursday , but if it is adjourned to another day you will let us know , as in that case Mrs. G . will go to Hampton . Pray let me have the names of Mr. Fisher 's eight to put in my list Yrs . ever H. M—— Miſs Hamilton 13 th - March 1785 H. More
