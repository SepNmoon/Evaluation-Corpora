My dear Miſs Hamilton -- My Mother thinks her self highly oblig 'd to you for yr Attention to her in writing so Civil a Note -- but she is not so Selfish a Woman as to wish you to refuse such an offer as that is -- of seeing Mrs Siddons my Mother & I hope to have the pleasure of seeing you very soon & we not only excuse you but desire you not to refuse a place -- for indeed she is worth seeing more than any Sight I ever saw -- Adieu my dear Miſs Hamilton -- I am yrs Affecly DBloſset Monday Morng Miſs Bloſset
