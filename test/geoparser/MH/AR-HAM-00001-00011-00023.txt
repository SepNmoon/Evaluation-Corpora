My Dr : Miſs Hamilton You said that Mrs : Delany designed me the Honor & Pleasure of a Visit -- I should be so happy to see her tomorrow Morning -- indeed it is the only Day I have at Liberty -- do settle it with her , & let me know -- my Sert : who brings this , can go on to Mrs : Delany & bring her answer . -- & if She can not come -- I will send for you Lord D will call upon you & bring you down -- Adieu Yrs : sincerely PDartrey Sunday Morng Miſs Hamilton Clargis Street
