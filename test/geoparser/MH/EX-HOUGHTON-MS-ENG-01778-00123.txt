My dear Miſs Hamilton Mrs. Garrick desires me to propose to you to be ready immediately after Church on Whitsunday June 8 th . and she will call on you about one o clock to take you to Hampton , and on tuesday She will return you to your own habitation safe and in good condition , the dangers of the Hounslow Heath excepted . You will let me have a line with your answer directed to Hampton , and pray send me all the news . Mrs. Garrick 's love . Have you heard from dear Mrs. Carter yet ? Yours dear Madam most affectionately H More 24 May 1783 To Miſs Hamilton Clarges Street Picadilly
