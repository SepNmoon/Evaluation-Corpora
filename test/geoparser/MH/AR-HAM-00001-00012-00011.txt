My Dear Miſs Hamilton , I did not receive your Note till late this Evening , but return you many thanks for it , as well as for your kindneſs to my little Creatures . I rejoice to hear their Rl . Hſs 's . are so well . We want nothing here to make our Séjour delightful , but better Weatheer ; the Rain has been almost inceſsant , & as yet has prevented our going once upon the Terrace . I am glad Mrs Schwellenberg is pretty well , & remain Ever my dear Miſs Hamilton Most Sincerely Yrs . CFinch Queens Lodge Windſor Monday Night . 5 th . July 1779 Miſs Hamilton Kew .
