Dear Miſs Hamilton I am Much obliged by Your kind Enquiries , I did not know You was returned till Yesterday Evening -- I have still a little Complaint in My Stomach ; & shall not go out , either tomorrow or Tuesday Evening -- I meant to have Requested the Favor of Your Company ; If you will let Me know Which will suit You Best ; I will send the Coach , as It is now at My Disposal ; Sr. Harry being out of Town ; -- I am dear Miſs Hamilton Yrs . Most Affecly- Frances Harpur Sunday --
