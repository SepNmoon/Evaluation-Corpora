Queens House Tuesday Evg 16 April 1781 I beg my dear you will acquaint Her Majesty with my Humble Duty that Doctor Turton aſsures me Princeſs Augusta continues gaining Ground , what I think is the most troublesome part of her Complaint at present , is that weasineſs which she feels allmost constantly , however our Walk was a little longer this Mng & we are just returned from a very fine Airing , H R H me to present her Duty to their Majesties & love to the Princeſs 's , with thanks to Prſs Elizabeth for her Letter . I hope P Royal has had no return of her Complaint , I beg my Affectionate duty to her & Prſs Elizth Adieu my Dear I hope you feel the good Effects of this charming Weather Affly Yr- MCGoldsworthy Princeſs Mary Princeſs Sophia & the two little Princes are perfectly well --
