15 Sept . Sunday 1781 Babel Dearest friend -- No post went out last night & therefore I could not vindicate my seeming neglect -- indeed my love I wrote on friday as I promised at Leuctra & gave the letter to Chs : Greville -- he I make no doubt forgot to send it -- if he has not yet done so -- pray send to his house in Portman Square -- I must not delay sending this to ye . post -- I will write to night for Tomorrow I return Bell s letter will talk of it bye & bye when I write adieu Sep tember
