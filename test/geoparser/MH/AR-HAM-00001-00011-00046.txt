Stanhope Street march 11 th 1806 madam I am Sorry I have delayd Sending the Silk so Long but when you hear the reason I hope you will excuse me , the day I received from yours poor mr wainwright was Dying and expired on Sunday morning the Second of this month his loſs is very much lamented by all who knew him and his poor wife is in the Deepest Distreſs my Good Lord and Lady are very much affected by it , and regret him very much , they beg their best wishes and h I have the Honor to be madam your Gratefull Servant EPalfrey I hope the Silk will go Safe Mrs Dickenson
