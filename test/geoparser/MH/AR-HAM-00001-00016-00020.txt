Dear Miſs Hamilton I enclose You a Pattern of My Gown ; I Bought It of a Man whose Name is Nunn ; lives in Ruſsell Court , Covent Garden ; He has very cheap Silks Blends & c ; -- does not I Believe keep a shop , but Brings Goods to any Who wishes to see them ; -- I am Much Better today -- Will offer Myself to You ; the first Eveng . I can ; but Have some Engagements for next Week ; Which I was obliged to put off , on Acct . of My Cold , which confined Me so long -- ever Yrs . Most Affecly -- Frances Harpur Saturday --
