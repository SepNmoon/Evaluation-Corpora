My Dear Miſs Hamilton I regret exceedingly that it has not yet been in my power to make you a visit I propose having that pleasure on Friday morning about eleven o'clock if agreeable to you , dont trouble yourself to write as I shall take for granted I may come if I do not hear from you -- Our dear Ly . Stormont & her little Boy go on as well as can be wished -- Believe me yrs . most affecly . Eliz . M. Murray Wednesday 11 th : August Miſs Hamilton Clarges Stt .
