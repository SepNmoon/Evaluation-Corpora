Blackheath May 21 st , 1773 I have just got yours My dearest Mary & propose setting off from London on Sunday ye 23 d in the afternoon & being wt you to dinner on ye Monday and shall be happy to meet you on the road riding but wont give Mrs Hamilton the trouble of coming a stage , as I am a very early man & I 'm not ſure but I may be wt you to breakfast so adieu My dearest Girl believe me yrs most affctly Napier To William Maule near Forfarshire Free Miſs Hamilton
