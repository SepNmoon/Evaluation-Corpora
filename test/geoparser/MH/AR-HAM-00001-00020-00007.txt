Long life & many happy Years to you Miſs . The Cane I received , & shall preserve it carefully . How could you imagine I did not intend going to Court to day ? I think you 're very Saucy . I not only go , but go in all my Glory . This Eveng- I return to Ly- Napier 's -- Another Matrimonial Scheme on the Tapis . £70,000 & a Park within 8 miles of London . Adieu Yours & c N—— 1 Janry 1779 ,
