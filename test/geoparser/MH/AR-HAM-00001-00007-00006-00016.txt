Mr. Fisher presents his Compliments to Miſs Hamilton , & returns her the Milkwoman s verses accompanied with the money for eight subscriptions Mr. F . took the Liberty of mentioning the subject to a certain great Lady , but found her already acquainted with all the Circumstances of the case , which had been communicated to her by Mrs. Delaney . Mr. F is very sorry he can not have the pleasure of seeing Miſs Hamilton this week , as the whole of his time is taken up with attendance , but hopes to have an opportunity of waiting upon her in the next week . -- S t .
