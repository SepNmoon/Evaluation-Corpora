My Drst : Miſs H . If you have any Charity you will come to us this Eveng : Ld.- D . Richd : & Mr : Penn & I long to see you -- we will tell you why when you come -- only come as soon as you can -- tell Mrs : H . I beg I entreat it as a great Favor -- never mind dreſs I am in my Dormeuse -- in haste Yrs : most truly PD you shall not stay late -- pray come if you poſsibly can -- Saturday -- I have no other Company & have got a sad Cold . I beg my best Compts : to Mrs : Hamilton To Miſs Hamilton James St : Wesm :
