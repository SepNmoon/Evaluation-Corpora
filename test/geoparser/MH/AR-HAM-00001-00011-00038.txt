From Lady Cremorne s Old faithful Servt . Mrs. Palfrey 7 th . April Stanhope Street Dear madam I am very much Concernd to hear of miſs Dickinson s Illneſs but will not mention it to night I have Sent the only thing I Could think of to night which was our Dear angels Cradle but if it will not do I Can get tomorrow from Chelsea a little Bed Compleat -- I have alſo Sent a pot of Currant Jelly as that may be of Service and any thing elſe that this house afford I need not say I am sure will be at mrs Dickenson s Service . I am madam your obedient Hum ble Sert E Palfrey
