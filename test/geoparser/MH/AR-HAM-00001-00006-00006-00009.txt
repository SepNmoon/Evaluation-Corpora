Dear friend How kind you are to think of your old woman and true friend in sending such a rarity ; for the poor birds who have so many enemies must be scarce . I thank you therefore for your love which I return from my heart . I have been for this six week a sick animal and kept my room : which by the by is no great hardship to me , if not in great pain and can amuse my mind . I suppose you are all well and lazy not to write a line to your true and faithful E : M : Garrick Hampton Saturday Octr : the 15 : To the family of love the Dickensons ' . at the si ng gn of comfort
