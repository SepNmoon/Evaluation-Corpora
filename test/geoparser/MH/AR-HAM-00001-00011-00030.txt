Dr : Sir I send inclosed a Line to Dr : Mrs : D , & Ly : Wake , & I most sincerely wish you a safe Journe y & that you will find all well at Courteen Hall -- I am sorry the Weather is so very severe , pray take care of yourself , & believe me Dr : Sir Your sincere & faithful Humbl : Sert PCremorne The good wishes of this Family attend you To John Dickinson Esqr Albemarle Street
