St James Place Monday Morn—— Mrs Delany s best Compliments to Miſs Hamelton and Desires to know how she does -- and whether she is to have the Company Pleasure of her Company to Chelsea to morrow morning if she is -- she will call Her in the Duchess of Portland s Coach -- at what Hour she thinks Proper -- and Desires she will let Her know by the Bearer -- Miſs Hamelton
